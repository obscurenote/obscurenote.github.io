Obscure

The privacy-first note app that stores notes locally with zero tracking—your thoughts stay on your device, always.

What is Obscure?

Obscure is your private space for notes and ideas—simple, fast, and truly yours. Everything you write stays on your device, never sent to the cloud or tracked. Enjoy a clean, distraction-free interface, instant search, and powerful editing tools, all designed to help you focus on what matters most: your thoughts.

Features

  • Local-only storage, zero tracking
  • AES-256 encrypted notes
  • Instant search
  • Lightning fast minimal UI
  • Rich text editing
  • Markdown support (planned)
  • LaTeX support (planned)
  • Tagging & organization (planned)
